cloud.gov.au is a Platform as a Service that makes it easy for teams to deploy their applications to the cloud.

Why we built it

We built cloud.gov.au to help government digital teams deliver products when working agile and trying to meet the Digital Service Standard.

Build apps not infrastrucutre

We beleve digital teams should focus on building great public services, not managing cloud infrastructure.

cloud.gov.au makes it easy for small digital teams to quickly deploy, manage and scale their own applications.

Meet user needs quickly

We know that to understand and meet user needs, teams need to be able to put updates, bug fixes and new features in the hands of users as quickly as possible.

cloud.gov.au makes deployment safe and easy, so teams can deploy updates to their applications in a matter of minutes.

Built for agile teams

We built cloud.gov.au with agile teams in mind. As agile practices become more common in Government, decision making and responsibility is being pushed down to the delivery team level. It is critical that we enable small teams to manage their own deployment and not rely on slow enterprise deployment process which can take weeks or months to deploy changes.

Read more about why we built cloud.gov.au

Industry standard technology, best-practice approach

cloud.gov.au is built with the industry leading Platform as a Service (PaaS) technology Cloud Foundry, running on top of Amazon Web Services in the Sydney region.
